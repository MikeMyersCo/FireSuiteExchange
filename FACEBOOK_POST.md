# Fire Suite Exchange - Now Testing!

We're testing a new platform to buy and sell Fire Suite tickets! Help us test it out!

## Quick Start (3 Easy Steps)

### STEP 1: Sign Up
Visit: https://firesuiteexchange.com
- Click "Login" then "Create an account"
- Enter email and password

### STEP 2: Get Seller Access
- Click "Become a Seller"
- Select your suite (Lower Bowl, North/South Terrace, or V101-V117)
- Upload proof (photo of your suite docs/tickets)
- Wait for approval (usually same day!)

### STEP 3: List Your Tickets
- Click "List Tickets"
- Enter event details, number of seats, and price
- Choose how buyers can contact you
- Done! Your tickets are now live
