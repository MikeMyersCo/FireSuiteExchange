# Fire Suite Exchange - Test User Instructions

## Getting Started & Listing Your Tickets

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Go to https://firesuiteexchange.com
2. Click "Login" in the top right
3. Click "Create an account"
4. Enter your email and create a password
5. Click "Sign Up"

### Step 2: Verify Your Suite Ownership
Before you can sell tickets, you need to verify you own a suite:

1. Click "Become a Seller" in the navigation menu
2. Select your suite area:
   - Lower Fire Suite (Suites 1-90)
   - Upper North Terrace (Suites 1-20)
   - Upper South Terrace (Suites 1-20)
   - V Sections (V101-V117)
3. Select your suite number from the dropdown
4. Upload proof of ownership:
   - Screenshot of your suite agreement
   - Photo of your suite ticket
   - Email confirmation from the venue
   - Any document showing your suite number
5. Add any additional notes (optional)
6. Click "Submit Application"

Wait for approval - An admin will review and approve your application (usually within 24 hours during testing)

### Step 3: List Your Tickets
Once approved, you can list tickets:

1. Click "List Tickets" in the navigation
2. Select the event you want to sell tickets for
3. Choose your suite from your verified suites
4. Enter ticket details:
   - Event date and time
   - Number of seats available (default: 8 for suites, 2 for V sections)
   - Price per seat (example: $50)
   - Seat numbers (if applicable)
5. Choose delivery method:
   - AXS Mobile Transfer (recommended)
   - Paper Tickets
   - PDF/Email
   - Will Call
6. Enter your contact information:
   - Email (auto-filled)
   - Phone number (optional)
   - Facebook Messenger username (optional)
7. Add any special notes for buyers
8. Click "Create Listing"

### Step 4: Manage Your Listings
- View all your listings at "My Listings"
- Edit listings by clicking the "Edit" button
- Mark tickets as sold when purchased
- Track how many views your listings get

## Browsing & Buying Tickets

### Finding Tickets
1. Go to "Browse Listings"
2. Filter by:
   - Search for specific artists/events
   - Click on the venue map to filter by suite area
   - Browse by date using the calendar
   - Sort by price (low to high, high to low)

### Contacting Sellers
When you find tickets you want:
1. Click on the listing to see full details
2. Use one of the contact methods:
   - Email Seller - Opens your email with ticket details pre-filled
   - Text Seller - Opens SMS to the seller's phone
   - Message on Messenger - Opens Facebook Messenger
   - Message Seller - Send an in-app message (if enabled)
