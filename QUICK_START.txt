FIRE SUITE EXCHANGE - QUICK START

SITE: https://firesuiteexchange.com

========================================

FOR SELLERS:

1. Sign Up
   - Go to firesuiteexchange.com
   - Login -> Create account

2. Become a Seller
   - Click "Become a Seller"
   - Select your suite area and number
   - Upload proof (photo of suite docs)
   - Submit and wait for approval

3. List Tickets
   - Click "List Tickets"
   - Fill in event, date, seats, price
   - Add contact info
   - Submit

========================================

FOR BUYERS:

1. Browse Listings
   - Click "Browse Listings"

2. Find Tickets
   - Search by artist name
   - Click venue map to filter by area
   - Sort by price

3. Contact Seller
   - Click on listing
   - Use Email/Text/Messenger buttons

========================================

SUITE AREAS:
- Lower Fire Suite (1-90)
- North Terrace (1-20)
- South Terrace (1-20)
- V Sections (V101-V117)
